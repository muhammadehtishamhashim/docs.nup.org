## Syllabus Covered

The midterm exam covers all topics from **Week 1** through **Week 8**. Below is a summary of the key concepts you should review:

### 1. Limits and Continuity (Week 1)
*   Functions of several variables
*   Limits and continuity of multivariable functions
*   Techniques for proving non-existence of limits

### 2. Partial Derivatives (Week 2)
*   Definition and computation of partial derivatives
*   Chain rule
*   Implicit differentiation

### 3. Multiple Integrals (Week 3)
*   Double integrals over rectangles and general regions
*   Double integrals in polar coordinates
*   Triple integrals (Cartesian, Cylindrical, Spherical coordinates)

### 4. Vector Calculus: Line Integrals (Weeks 4-5)
*   Line integrals of scalar functions
*   Line integrals of vector fields (Work)
*   Fundamental Theorem for Line Integrals
*   Surface Integrals and Flux

### 5. Vector Calculus: Theorems (Week 6)
*   Green's Theorem (Plane)
*   Stokes' Theorem (Surface to Boundary Curve)

### 6. Differential Equations & Modeling (Weeks 7-8)
*   Periodic Functions and Laplace Transforms
*   Spring/Mass Systems: Driven Motion
*   Resonance and Beats Phenomenon
